What is EvE Online?

EvE Online is a MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game) that sets itself apart from the rest by being entirely player driven. Save a few activities, everything in the game is done by other players, from mining materials to items sold on market. Even the ships, weapons, and ammunition are made and traded by players. There are many different activities a player can take part in, in this massive sandbox environment, but the bottom line is, anything goes in EvE from merchant scamming to pirates, from megacorporations to small manufacturing ventures, Isk(in game money) is the name of the game.

Types of Gameplay

There are many types of gameplay in EvE online, they include:

  • Mining
  • PvP Combat
  • PvE Missions
  • Pirating
  • Manufacturing
  • Exploration
  • Salvaging
  • And so much more!
EvE online is such a large player driven game that its easy to get overwhelmed with the scope of activities and content available. A good resource for learning how and what to do in game is the EvE University website.
